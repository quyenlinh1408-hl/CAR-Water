# CAR-Water

Game Unity ve chu de o to tham hiem duoi dai duong.

## Mo ta nhanh
- Nguoi choi dieu khien xe tham hiem o moi truong day bien.
- He thong `WaterproofManager` mo phong trang thai trong cabin kin nuoc va khi ra ngoai moi truong nuoc.
- Da thiet lap san day bien (sand floor) va tong the anh sang xanh dai duong.

## Dieu khien hien tai
- Nhan `Play` trong Unity Editor de chay demo.
- Nhan phim `F` de chuyen giua 2 che do:
  - `Inside car`: tat suong mu.
  - `Outside underwater`: bat suong mu xanh duoi nuoc.
